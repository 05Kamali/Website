🌾 Steps to Cultivate Cereals 🌾

1. Land Preparation 🚜

Plough the field, level it, and apply compost or manure to enrich the soil and prepare a good seedbed.

2. Seed Selection 🌾

Select high-yielding, disease-resistant, and certified seeds suitable for the crop and region.

3. Seed Treatment 💊

Treat seeds with fungicides or bio-fertilizers before sowing to protect against early diseases.

4. Sowing / Transplanting 🌱

Sow directly or transplant seedlings depending on the crop (e.g., transplant for paddy).

5. Irrigation 💧

Provide regular watering. For rice, maintain standing water; for wheat, irrigate at intervals.

6. Weeding 🌿

Remove weeds regularly to prevent competition for nutrients using hand tools or herbicides.

7. Fertilization 🧪

Apply appropriate doses of NPK fertilizers or organic manure based on crop needs and soil testing.

8. Pest & Disease Control 🐛

Monitor for pests and diseases. Use eco-friendly or recommended pesticides when needed.

9. Harvesting 🧺

Harvest when grains are fully mature, dry, and hard. Use sickles or harvesters based on scale.

10. Post-Harvest Handling 🏚️

Dry the grains properly and store them in clean, dry, and pest-free containers or silos.

🌿 Happy Farming! Grow Fresh, Eat Fresh! 🌿
